Pizza Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Pepperoni
  • Feta Cheese
  • Jalepenos

Pizza Instructions

  1. Spread pizza sauce on pizza dough
  2. Put mozzerella on top of the sauce
  3. Put pepperoni on top of the mozzerella
  4. Put jalepenos on top of the mozzerella and pepperoni
  5. Sprinkle feta cheese across the pizza
  6. Cook in oven
  7. Eat when ready
